# Generative AI Multimedia Website: Comprehensive Asset Generation Guide

This document contains the exact blueprints, tools, and prompts required to generate all the physical media assets (Images, Audio, Videos) needed to complete **Milestone 4** of the website.

Once generated, save these files in your `assets/` subdirectories and replace our temporary placeholder names.

---

## 1. Global & Structural Images

### 1.1 Website Logo

- **Target Path:** `assets/images/logo/logo.webp` (or `.svg`)
- **Where it's used:** Global Navigation Bar (top left) and Footer.
- **Recommended Tool:** Canva (for transparency/text) or Midjourney / DALL-E 3 (for the icon).
- **Prompt (DALL-E 3):**
  > "A minimalist, modern technology logo vector icon combining a glowing neural network brain with a camera lens and a soundwave. Flat vector style, dark navy background, with bright cyan and neon purple accents. Clean, professional tech startup aesthetic without any text or letters."
- **Post-Processing:** Use a tool like Photopea to remove the dark background and save as a transparent PNG, then convert to WEBP.

### 1.2 Home Page Hero Image

- **Target Path:** `assets/images/hero/hero-placeholder.webp` _(rename to `hero-image.webp`)_
- **Where it's used:** `index.html` (The massive visual on the right side of the main title).
- **Recommended Tool:** DALL-E 3 or Midjourney.
- **Prompt (DALL-E 3):**
  > "A futuristic digital landscape showing artificial intelligence generating multimedia. Show abstract glowing neural networks intertwining with transparent digital screens displaying a glowing camera, a musical note, and code. Modern tech aesthetic, dark space background, vibrant neon cyan and purple gradients, high contrast, 3D render quality."

### 1.3 Traditional vs. Generative AI Diagram

- **Target Path:** `assets/images/sections/traditional-vs-genai.webp`
- **Where it's used:** `about.html` (Next to the Traditional vs. GenAI comparison cards).
- **Recommended Tool:** Canva (Highly recommended for text infographics) or DALL-E 3.
- **Prompt (If using DALL-E 3 to generate the background elements):**
  > "A sleek infographic illustration split into two sides. Left side represents 'Traditional AI' featuring a mechanical funnel sorting colorful data blocks. Right side represents 'Generative AI' featuring a glowing magic wand bursting with colorful, newly created 3D objects like a picture frame, a music note, and a video clapperboard. Dark tech theme, neon cyan and purple lighting."

## 3. Audio & Voice Assets

### 3.1 AI Voice Narration Track

- **Target Path:** `assets/audio/sample-1.mp3`
- **Where it's used:** `gallery.html` (The audio player under the "AI Voice Narration" section).
- **Recommended Tool:** ElevenLabs (Free tier available)
- **How to create:**
  1. Create an ElevenLabs account.
  2. Go to Text-to-Speech.
  3. Select a "Professional/Narrative" voice (e.g., Adam, Rachel, or Marcus).
  4. Set Stability to high (~75%) for a consistent educational tone.
- **Target Script (Copy-paste this as the "Prompt"):**
  > "Welcome to the future of multimedia production. Generative AI is no longer just a conceptual theory; it is actively reshaping how we compose images, write scripts, and direct videos. Listen closely to this voice�it was entirely generated by a neural network trained on human speech, serving as direct proof of how far artificial intelligence has evolved."

## 4. Video Demonstrations

### 4.1 "How Generative AI Works" Video

- **Target Path:** `assets/videos/demos/how-it-works.mp4` _(Upload to YouTube and embed, or host locally and update the iframe inside `gallery.html`)_.
- **Where it's used:** `gallery.html` (At the bottom, replacing the default YouTube embed iframe).
- **Recommended Tool Stack:**
  1. **Runway (Gen-2) or Pika:** Generate short 4-second video clips.
  2. **ElevenLabs:** Generate the voiceover audio.
  3. **CapCut (Desktop):** Stitch the clips and audio together, and use CapCut's "Auto-Captions" feature to strictly satisfy the PRD requirement for subtitles.
- **Prompts for Video Clips (Runway/Pika):**
  - _Clip 1:_ "A glowing digital brain pulsing with neon light, camera panning around it."
  - _Clip 2:_ "A human hand typing on a glowing holographic keyboard in the dark."
  - _Clip 3:_ "Digital particles rapidly forming into a beautiful landscape."
- **Voice Script to generate in ElevenLabs and lay over the final video:**
  > "Generative AI follows a simple but powerful pipeline. First, the model trains on millions of data points to learn patterns. Then, a human provides a creative text prompt. The AI translates this text into data, generating entirely novel multimedia outputs like the video you are watching right now. Finally, the human refines the result for perfect production quality."
- _Note: In `gallery.html`, update the `<iframe>` URL to match your final video._

## Asset Format Guidelines (PRD Constraints)

1. **Images:** MUST be converted to `.webp` or `.avif` format before placing them in the project folders. (Use a free tool like _CloudConvert_ or _Photopea_ if DALL-E outputs PNGs).
2. **Audio:** Use standard compressed `.mp3`.
3. **Video Subtitles:** Your final video MUST have hardcoded subtitles. Use CapCut's Auto-Caption feature to generate them easily.
